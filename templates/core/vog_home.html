PEOPLE WILL STARE.

THEIR WHILE.

Vogue Nation, the fashion designing module, newly concieved by the team of Alcheringa 2020 is an attempt to begin the search for the all round champion of the Fashion Designing arena. This will be the race of the elite of the elite. This is where you begin your trek to the top. Three best of the best events especially tailored for the ones destined for greatness. Etch your name into the GOLDEN history of Alcheringa as the ultimate champion of the Fashion Module.

HAUTE COUTURE

Haute Couture, where the most glamorous people show off their skills as they walk the ramp and evoking all those creative people out there to put on their thinking caps and come out with the most innovative theme based fashion show.

TIME

The Pre-finals have a 30-minute time limit with a specific theme, while the finals, lasting 45 minutes, are theme-less.

VENUE

The Haute Couture competition will be hosted at the IIT Guwahati Auditorium, radiating elegance.

TEAM COMPOSITION

Every Team has to have minimum 5 models, utmost 4 designers, 1 choreographer and 1 make-up artist. The minimum age limit for models is 17 years.

STATES OF DRESS

States of Dress, where you let your creativity run wild as you design the best outfit for your friend using only paper. Put your origami and fashion skills to use and come up with the perfect dress.

TIME

The time limit for the event is set at 3 hours, and the theme will be revealed on the spot.

VENUE

The States of Dress competition is scheduled to take place on the IIT Guwahati campus, with the precise venue details to be communicated shortly.

TEAM COMPOSITION

Each team will have 3 members, one person, for whom the outfit will be designed and the others designer for the outfit

RULE BOOK

GLAMOUR NOVA

Glamour Nova is the stage where the most glamorous individuals showcase their skills through elegant photoshoots, set against exotic locations of their choosing.

TIME

This is an online competition so the submission of photos must be done well before 1st March

VENUE

As an online competition, participants are required to conduct their photoshoots at five distinct locations, featuring a new outfit each time. Submissions can be made at vogue.alcheringa.in.

TEAM COMPOSITION

Every Team has to have 2 models (1 male and 1 female), utmost 2 designers and 1 photographer. Minimum age limits for models is 17 years.
